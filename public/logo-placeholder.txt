Reality Auditor Logo Placeholder
================================

To add your logo:

1. Add your logo2.JPG file to the public folder
2. Rename it to logo.png for better browser compatibility
3. The dashboard and site will automatically use it

For favicon generation:
- Use the script at scripts/generate-favicon.js
- Or use online tools like favicon.io/favicon-converter/

The logo should be the eye+fingerprint Reality Auditor branding.
